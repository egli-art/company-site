About Client

LevelField Financial is a US-based fintech company that seeks to unite digital assets with traditional finance into its platform. Their mission is to provide a full suite of products and services that will cover all digital assets and traditional banking needs of their clients.

Obstacles

LevelField Financial were looking to rebrand their website with new user interface as well as launch their new products and services (web and mobile applications) by Fall 2022. They needed a company that would help with the implementation of their rebranding system into a high-quality website that could provide a better user experience.

Actions

The team worked together to materialize the new design into a fast and high-performing website, by showcasing the company's products and allowing the new users to join the waitlist for their launch date.

Result

The new website of LevelField is up and launched at levelfield.us
