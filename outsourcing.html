We work within two models of outsourcing ​

Staff Augmentation

    Staff augmentation or outstaffing - you hire a developer (or a few of them) that will join your team. You will receive a dev with all of the functions of one of your employees, but having been officially recruited by NetInteractive. We will be responsible for paying wages and taxes, and you will have direct communication with the dev and will be able to manage and control the working process at every stage.​

Project Outsourcings

    The idea behind outsourcing is to complete a specific project. This means if you need help with your project you can entrust us with certain tasks or the whole project development. You don't have to communicate directly with the development team, we offer a project manager with whom you will manage the working process. So there is no need for you to be involved in every stage of the project. ​
